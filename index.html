Common questions.

How does the bundle pricing work?
Starter is pay-as-you-go at $129 per role. Growth and Core are pre-purchased bundles (5 and 12 roles respectively) valid for 12 months. Use your roles whenever you need them — no monthly fees when you're not actively hiring. Bundles never expire mid-hire.
Is ROLEIQ's psychometric assessment scientifically validated?
ROLEIQ uses a forced-choice OCEAN-based assessment purpose-built for recruitment. It's designed to be role-specific, AI-resistant, and produce explainable scoring. For roles requiring certified clinical-grade assessment, Deep Dive adds the Birkman Method — backed by 70+ years of validation research.
What's the difference between ROLEIQ and a traditional psychometric test?
Traditional tools (like Birkman, Hogan, or SHL) are general-purpose instruments requiring certified practitioners and per-candidate fees of $150–$600+. ROLEIQ is designed specifically for recruitment decisions — faster, scalable, and integrated with role generation. Deep Dive bridges both worlds for critical hires.
When should I use Deep Dive versus ROLEIQ Core?
Core is the right tool for the majority of roles. Deep Dive is recommended when the cost of a bad hire is significant — typically leadership, senior specialist, or culture-defining roles where you want certified psychometric depth on your shortlisted candidates.
Is there a free trial?
We offer a live demo on a real role from your business — not a slideshow. Book a 20-minute demo and we'll run ROLEIQ on an actual open role so you can see exactly what you get before committing.
How quickly can I get started?
Same day. Once your account is activated, your first role can be live in under 5 minutes. The AI generates the full role package — description, job ad, and psychometric benchmark — automatically from a job title.
