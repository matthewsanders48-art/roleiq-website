How it works

From role brief to ranked
shortlist in three steps.

01

Define the role

Enter the job title, responsibilities, and select your assessment depth — Essential, Standard, or Comprehensive. ROLEIQ generates the full role brief and ideal personality profile automatically.

02

Candidates self-assess

Each candidate completes a tailored psychometric assessment — 30 AI-resistant OCEAN personality questions plus optional intellectual reasoning tests. Takes 15–45 minutes.

03

Get your ranked shortlist

ROLEIQ scores every candidate against the role's ideal profile. You get a ranked shortlist with full OCEAN breakdowns, cognitive scores, and AI-generated hiring recommendations.
